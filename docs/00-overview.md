# 00. Overview — What is Listing Forge and what am I getting into?

**Read time: 5 minutes**

Hey. Welcome. Before we touch any settings or buttons, let's set expectations.

## What you're building

You're going to set up a small program on a computer at your house (or rented online) that does this:

1. You open a website (the **GUI** — graphical user interface)
2. You drag a photo of your handmade item into it
3. You type one sentence describing what it is
4. You click "Forge listing"
5. About 90 seconds later, a complete Etsy and Shopify listing appears on the screen

That's it. You copy the parts you want into your actual Etsy and Shopify shops and publish.

## Why three AIs and not just ChatGPT?

Each AI has different strengths:

- **Google's Gemini** is best at looking at pictures and at searching the web for current info
- **Anthropic's Claude** is best at writing in someone's natural voice without sounding like AI slop
- **OpenAI's ChatGPT** is best at quick, punchy short-form stuff like ad headlines and tags

Using one AI for everything is like asking a sculptor to also do your bookkeeping. They might do it, but it won't be their best work.

## What you actually need

**Required:**

1. **A computer that stays on all the time.** This can be:
   - A NAS like Synology, QNAP, or a similar always-on box
   - An old laptop or desktop you don't use anymore
   - A Raspberry Pi 4 or 5
   - A cheap rented online server (called a "VPS") for $5–7/month
2. **An internet connection** at that computer
3. **About $15** to start funding the AI accounts
4. **30 to 60 minutes** of focused time

**Helpful but not required:**

- Some comfort with following step-by-step instructions
- Knowing how to copy and paste things
- A second screen so you can have these docs open while you work

## What you don't need

- You **don't need to know how to code**. The instructions are step by step. You'll be copy-pasting, clicking buttons, and filling in forms.
- You **don't need a powerful computer**. The AI processing happens on the AI companies' servers, not yours. Your computer is just running a switchboard.
- You **don't need a fast internet connection**. Anything that can stream Netflix is more than enough.

## How much will it actually cost?

The software is free. Always free. Open source.

What costs money is the AI usage. You pay the AI companies directly when their AI does work for you. There's no middleman.

Real costs based on actual use:

- **5 listings a week**: about $5/month total
- **20 listings a week**: about $12/month total
- **50 listings a week**: about $25/month total

You also need to fund each AI account up front. Minimum is usually $5 each, so **$15 total to start**. That $15 will last most makers a couple months.

## How long will the whole setup take?

If everything goes smoothly: **30 minutes**.
If you hit a snag: **60–90 minutes**.

The longest part is usually getting the API keys (we'll explain what those are) because each AI company makes you create an account and verify your email.

## What if I get stuck?

Each section of these docs has a "If something went wrong" box at the bottom. There's also [docs/08-troubleshooting.md](08-troubleshooting.md) at the end that covers the most common issues.

If you're really stuck, you can open an issue on the GitHub repo and someone will help.
